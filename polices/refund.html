Refund Policy

At Bell Photographers, our goal is to make sure that every family and student is satisfied with their portraits. If you're not satisfied with your portraits, and a retake date is available at your school, Bell Photographers will retake your photo (Fall Only). All that you need to do is bring the portraits with you on retake day and return it to the photographer. If a retake date is not available at your school, you may request a refund.

Complete satisfaction is guaranteed with Bell Photographers. We will refund full payment if all portraits are returned within 10 days of receipt.
